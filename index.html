003 · About

Six years. One city.
One craft.

I am Odbat Batsaikhan — colorist, filmmaker, and tool developer based in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia. Six years building a practice in color grading, DCTL development, and creative systems for independent filmmakers.

"Your color grade isn't invisible because it's subtle. It's invisible because it's honest."

NODX started as a studio. Now it's four interconnected verticals — creative agency, creator academy, community, and talent — built for Mongolian and Asian filmmakers serious about their work.
