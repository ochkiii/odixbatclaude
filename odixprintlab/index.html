N°06 · Process
Three steps
Step 01 — Get
Send "LUT" to @odixbat

Open Instagram, DM the word LUT to @odixbat. The download link arrives directly in your messages — no email, no form, no wait.

Step 02 — Import
Drop the .cube files

In DaVinci: Color Management → LUT folder → paste files → refresh. In Premiere: Lumetri Color → Creative → Browse to your .cube files.

Step 03 — Grade
Apply at 60–80%

Expose your log footage correctly before applying. Start at 70% opacity. Each LUT name tells you its intent — trust it, then refine to taste.
