N°03 How to use
Step 01
DM the keyword

Send "LUT" to @odixbat on Instagram. You'll get the download link directly. No email. No form.

Step 02
Download and import

Unzip the pack. Import the .cube files into DaVinci Resolve via the LUT folder, or use the Creative Looks panel in Premiere.

Step 03
Grade at 50–70%

Apply to your log or flat footage. Start at 70% opacity and adjust exposure before the LUT. Each LUT is named for its intent — trust the intent.
